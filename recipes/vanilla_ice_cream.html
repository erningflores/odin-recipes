Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

Imagine you are doing a low carbohydrates diet and you can still eat an ice cream.

This recipe uses a "no-churn" method, so you don't need any special equipment!

  • Yields: About 4 servings
  • Prep time: 10 minutes
  • Freeze time: At least 4 hours

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream (full fat)
  • 2 tablespoons powdered erythritol or your preferred powdered low-carb sweetener
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • pinch of salt
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon of vodka or other clear alcohol (helps prevent it from freezing rock solid, you won't taste it)

Steps:

  1. Chill a container: Place a freezer-safe container (glass or plastic) in the freezer while you prepare the mixture.
  2. Combine ingredients: In a medium bowl, combine the heavy whipping cream, powdered sweetener, vanilla extract, and salt. If using, add the vodka.
  3. Whip until stiff peaks form: Use an electric mixer (handheld or stand mixer) to whip the mixture until stiff peaks form, similar to whipped cream. This incorporates air and helps create a creamy texture.
  4. Transfer to chilled container: Pour the whipped cream mixture into the chilled freezer-safe container.
  5. Freeze: Cover the container with a lid or plastic wrap (pressing it directly onto the surface of the ice cream to prevent ice crystals) and freeze for at least 4 hours, or preferably overnight, until solid
  6. Soften and serve: Before serving, let the ice cream sit at room temperature for about 10-15 minutes to soften slightly for easier scooping.
